# Role: Operability Support

## What This Role Cares About

- operational ownership
- support and admin handling after release
- operational readiness assumptions
- boundaries between capability behavior and supporting operations

## Questions To Ask

- Who owns this capability operationally?
- What support or admin actions might be required?
- Are handoffs between product, support, and operations clear enough?
- Is operational readiness implied but not stated?

## Common Gaps

- no named operational owner
- missing support/admin expectations
- capability behavior defined without operational handling
- support and serviceability concerns split inconsistently

## Avoid

- writing full runbooks
- assigning ownership without evidence
- turning this role into delivery planning
