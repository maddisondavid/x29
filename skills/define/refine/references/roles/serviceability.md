## Questions To Ask

- How will support or operations identify and investigate failures?
- What diagnostic information would be needed?
- Are admin or support workflows implied?
- Are support responsibilities assumed but unstated?

## Common Gaps

- no support workflow described
- missing diagnostics expectations
- failure handling mentioned without support implications
- unclear operational ownership

## Avoid

- designing runbook steps in detail
- inventing monitoring implementation
- expanding into incident-management process design
