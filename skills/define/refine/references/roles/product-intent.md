# Role: Product Intent

## What This Role Cares About

- clear statement of the capability need
- the user or business problem being addressed
- outcome clarity without solution design drift
- alignment between summary, description, scope, and acceptance criteria

## Questions To Ask

- What user or business problem is this capability solving?
- What outcome matters for this pass?
- Is the capability described in business language rather than design detail?
- Are success conditions implied but not written down?

## Common Gaps

- vague problem statement
- capability framed as a solution rather than a need
- mismatch between summary and fuller description
- missing functional acceptance criteria

## Avoid

- designing the solution
- inventing stakeholder consensus
- expanding into architecture or delivery plans
