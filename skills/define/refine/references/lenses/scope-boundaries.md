# Lens: Scope Boundaries

## What This Lens Cares About

- clear in-scope and out-of-scope boundaries
- hidden assumptions at capability edges
- v1 versus later-phase separation
- avoiding accidental sprawl

## Usually Updates

- `01-scope.md`

- `acceptance-criteria/functional.md`

## Questions To Ask

- What is explicitly in scope for this pass?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- Where are the unclear edges or implied exclusions?
- Are future-phase ideas being mixed into the current Define package?

## Common Gaps

- missing out-of-scope statements
- hidden boundary assumptions
- blurred lines between capability intent and future enhancement ideas
- acceptance criteria that exceed stated scope

## Avoid

- turning uncertainty into arbitrary exclusions
- redefining the capability beyond the requested pass
- drifting into planning or slicing
