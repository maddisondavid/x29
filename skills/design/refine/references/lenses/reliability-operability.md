# Lens: reliability-operability

## Focus

Assess whether the design can be run, supported, and recovered in production.

## Prioritize

- failure-mode awareness and graceful degradation
- operability responsibilities and on-call implications
- maintenance burden and supportability constraints

## Typical Files

- `03-solution-blueprint.md`
- `04-risks-and-tradeoffs.md`
- `05-open-questions.md`

## Questions To Probe

- Are likely failure modes identified with mitigation direction?
- Can operators diagnose and recover from common incidents?
- Is ownership for support and operations clear?
