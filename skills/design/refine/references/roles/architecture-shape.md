# Role: architecture-shape

## Focus

Strengthen the structural shape of the solution: service boundaries, ownership, and interaction flow.

## Prioritize

- coherent boundaries and responsibility splits
- consistency with known platform constraints
- clear explanation of where state and control live

## Typical Files

- `03-solution-blueprint.md`
- `02-decision.md`
- `04-risks-and-tradeoffs.md`

## Questions To Probe

- Are boundaries clear enough for impact mapping?
- Does the design over-centralize or over-fragment responsibility?
- Are integration seams explicit and testable?
