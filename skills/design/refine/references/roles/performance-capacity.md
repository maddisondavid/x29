# Role: performance-capacity

## Focus

Test the design against expected load, latency, throughput, and growth constraints.

## Prioritize

- known performance targets and workload assumptions
- bottleneck hypotheses and scaling strategy
- tradeoffs between simplicity, cost, and performance headroom

## Typical Files

- `01-options-considered.md`
- `03-solution-blueprint.md`
- `04-risks-and-tradeoffs.md`

## Questions To Probe

- Are workload assumptions explicit and realistic?
- Which components are likely to bottleneck first?
- Are performance risks visible enough before planning?
